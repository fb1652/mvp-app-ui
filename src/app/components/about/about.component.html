Features

  • See a view of your hardware configurations and major peripherals within a CPC, including:
    • Total number of processors and their allocations across your SYSPLEX/LPARS
    • Serial number for each processor
    • Type of processor
  • See data about software in your environment, including:
    • Operating systems
    • Subsystems
    • Registered products
    • Vendor products
  • Track the health of your hardware and software in one location. With MRI, view exception information for each LPAR you assess, by:
    • Owner
    • Type of check
    • Severity level (high, medium, and low)

How MRI Can Benefit You

By providing a detailed view of the hardware and software in your complex, MRI gives you intelligence you can use to optimize your environment and potentially reduce your cost.

How It Works

Mainframe Resource Intelligence (MRI) allows you to easily and frequently run scans of your mainframe configuration to recommend improvements to optimize cost savings, reliability, processes, and security.
